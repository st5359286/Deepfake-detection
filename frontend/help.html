❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is deepfake detection? ▼
Deepfake detection is the process of identifying manipulated media (images, videos, or audio) that have been artificially created using artificial intelligence. Our tool analyzes various indicators like facial inconsistencies, metadata, and visual artifacts to determine if content is authentic or manipulated.
How accurate is the detection? ▼
Our detection engine achieves high accuracy rates, typically between 85-95% for clear manipulations. However, accuracy may vary depending on the quality of the media and the sophistication of the manipulation technique used.
What file types are supported? ▼
We support images (JPG, PNG, GIF, TIFF), videos (MP4, AVI, MOV), and audio files (WAV, MP3). Maximum file size is 50MB for best performance.
Is my data secure? ▼
Yes! We take data security seriously. Your uploaded files are processed securely and automatically deleted from our servers after analysis. We never share your data with third parties.
How does live detection work? ▼
Live detection uses your device's camera to analyze video in real-time. It detects faces and analyzes them for signs of manipulation. Perfect for video calls and live streams.
Can I download analysis reports? ▼
Yes! After each analysis, you can download a detailed PDF report that includes confidence scores, feature analysis, and metadata information.
How do I reset my password? ▼
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
Why do I need to verify my email? ▼
Email verification ensures the security of your account and allows us to send you important notifications about your analyses. It's a required security step.
